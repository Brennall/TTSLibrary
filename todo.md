# Ship List

Every Faction should have at least 2 of the following ship classes in order to qualify as "sufficient"
- 2x Frigate
- 2x Destroyer
- 2x Light Cruiser
- 2x Heavy Cruiser
- 1x Battleship

(small craft)
- 1x Shuttlecraft
- 2x Fighters
- 1x Gunship

(stations & civilian craft)
- 1x Fortress
- 1x Outpost
- 1x Large Freighter
- 1x Small Freighter

(optional)
- 1x Tug (variant of Light Cruiser)
